AI Ethical Concerns and Situations

The implementation of AI into usable or entertaining tools seems to have unlimited potential. It can be found in health devices, help find answers to simple questions on the internet, create logos and images, generate ideas, create memes, and a ton more. As AI is continuing to become more advanced and prevalent in ordinary citizens daily life, it is critical to acknowledge, address, and find a solution to AI’s ethical concerns. There is widespread concern about potential and already realized misuse and abuse of AI. This extends itself to “Deep Fake” technology and the general spreading of misinformation or disinformation through AI channels. Resolutions to these issues won’t come easy, however, acknowledging them and making everyday people aware of thesm will arm people with the knowledge needed to better navigate problematic situations created by AI.
